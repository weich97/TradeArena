# Trajectory Audit Task 001

Use `skills/tradearena-trajectory-audit/SKILL.md`.

Inspect a trajectory and report:

- raw decision versus approved decision differences;
- risk edits, blocks, or violations;
- rejected, delayed, or partially filled orders;
- whether the artifact supports an engineering, benchmark, or scientific claim;
- the next command needed to reproduce the finding.

Do not make buy, sell, or profitability recommendations.
