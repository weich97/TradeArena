# Risk Budget Checklist

Review these fields before making a conclusion:

- `max_position_weight`
- `max_gross_exposure`
- `max_single_step_turnover`
- `max_drawdown`
- `max_order_participation`
- `min_confidence`
- `max_latency_steps`
- `max_slippage_bps`

Check that severe violations are not described as ordinary performance
differences.
