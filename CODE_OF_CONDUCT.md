# Code of Conduct

TradeArena follows the Contributor Covenant spirit: be respectful, assume good
faith, and keep technical disagreements focused on evidence, reproducibility,
and user safety.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Critique code, data, assumptions, and claims rather than people.
- Respect privacy, credential safety, and provider terms.
- Clearly label financial experiments as research artifacts, not advice.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private keys, broker credentials, raw provider caches, or private
  portfolio data.
- Encouraging unattended live trading through public examples.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or
artifacts that violate these norms. Security-sensitive reports should follow
`SECURITY.md`.
